# Bergische Universität Wuppertal, Chair of Fire Dynamics (BUW)

The data has been recorded at the Chair of Inorganic Chemistry and Fire Dynamics at the Bergische Universität Wuppertal.Team: Björn Beele, Frauke Binder, Adam Slabon and Fabian Brännström

## Experimental Conditions: Simultaneous Thermal Analysis (STA)

The sample powder was provided by MaCFP in a small glass bottle.Upon arrival, the bottle was opened and placed inside a desiccator for storage.The samples were weighed at room condition and stored at room condition prior to measurement. No pre-conditioning was applied.

The data was interpolated to match a 0.5 K spacing, as requested by MaCFP.Note that this can lead to minor inconsistencies for a few initial data pointswhere the temperature is not yet monotonically increasing, due to noise.
