# IMT Mines Alès (IMT)

### Experimental Conditions: TGA
A Perkin Elmer TGA 8000 was used to conduct Thermogravimetric Analysis (TGA) experiments. 

* Heating Rates: 2, 5, 10, 20 K/min
* Temperature program
  - Initial Temperature: 303 K
  - Initial Isotherm: 120 s (hold at 303 K for 120 s)
  - Heating Ramp: from 303 to 383 K at 10 K/min
  - Second Isotherm: 600 s (hold at 383 K for 600 s)
  - Maximum Temperature: 1173 K
  - Final Isotherm: None
* Sample mass: 5 mg +/- 0.1 mg
* Sample geometry: Powdered
* Calibration type: Before each experiment, a baseline test was performed using an empty crucible. 
* Crucible
  - Type: alumina
  - Volume: 18 uL
  - Diameter: 6 mm
  - Mass: 140 mg
  - Lid: No lid
* Carrier Gas
  - Type: Nitrogen or air
  - Flow rate: 20 ml/min
* Instrument
  - Type: Perkin Elmer TGA 8000
  - Furnace: Platinum + ceramic protective coating
* Notes
  - The temperature reported in the data files corresponds to the sample temperature and not the furnace temperature

### Experimental Conditions: MCC
A FTT microscale combustion calorimeter was used to conduct MCC experiments. The residual solid yield was measured after each test.

* Heating Rate: 60 K/min
* Temperature program (Pyrolyzer)
  - Initial temperature: between 380 and 420 K
  - Isotherm: up to O2 stabilization  
  - Maximum temperature: 1023 K
* Combustor Temperature: 1173K
* Sample mass: between 2.2 and 3.2 mg +/- 0.1mg
* Sample geometry: Powdered
* Calibration type: O2 sensor/flow calibration was checked at the start of each week of testing
* Crucible
  - Type: alumina
  - Volume: 16 uL
  - Diameter: 5 mm
  - Mass: 170 mg
  - Lid: No lid
* Pyrolyzer carrier gas
  - Type: N2/O2 mixture at 100/0, 98/2, 95/5, 90/10, or 80/20
  - Flow rate: 100 ml/min
* Combustor carrier gas (when pyrolyzer gas is pure nitrogen)
  - Type: N2/O2 mixture at 80/20
  - Flow rate: 100 ml/min
* Instrument
  - Type: FTT
  - O2 Analyzer: Chemical
