Cone Calorimeter experiments were conducted on an FTT iCone Mini (West Sussex, UK) at 1 heat flux (50 kW/m2) with an exhaust flow of 24 L/s using the standardized cone calorimeter procedure (ASTM E1354-25). ##### Test Summary

Cone Calorimeter experiments were conducted on an FTT iCone Mini (West Sussex, UK) at 1 heat flux (50 kW/m2) with an exhaust flow of 24 L/s using the standardized cone calorimeter procedure (ASTM E1354-25). Samples were wrapped in aluminum foil per the ASTM E1354 standard and placed upon ceramic wool insulation for testing. The optional retainer frame from ASTN 1354 was used. Thermocouples (Omega, GG-K-30-SLE) were installed on the top surface, 8.6 mm from top, and 17.2 mm from top. The thermocouples that were embedded were installed by drilling 45 mm-deep pockets with diameter of on opposite edges of the block so the thermocouple wire could be inserted parallel to the isotherm. The thermocouple wires were run out the top comer of the retainer frame. The thermocouples will affect the mass.
